Chicken-rice

Description

Easiest high protien and low effort meal that could me made in a matter of 10 mins. It is so easy to make yet so delicious.It is also perfect for meal prep and batch cooking.

It is very low effort and also budget friendly, With the money of a single restuarant meal, you could cook upto to 4 of these meal. It also incorporates all of the required nutrients.It is a template recipie meaning you could customize it in any manner

Ingredients

  • Onion - 1
  • Carot - 2
  • Cabbage - 1/4
  • Chicken breast - 250grams
  • Boiled Rice - 300grams
  • Biryani masala

Steps

  1. Boil Rice - 1 cup of rice requires 2 cups of water.
  2. Grate carots and cabbage
  3. Boil Chicken for about 8 minutes
  4. Add 2 tbsp of oil to a pan
  5. Add chopped onions to the pan
  6. Add the grated cabbage and carrots to the pan
  7. Add rice and chicken
  8. Add biriyani masala - (less calorie and more flavour) and saute
  9. Add salt and pepper
  10. Saute for a while unitl all the Ingredients are mixed homogenously
  11. All throught the process, cook in high heat
